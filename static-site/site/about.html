About Our Team

Security engineering, compliance, and incident response specialists

Leadership

Joseph Miller, Founder & CEO

View Joseph Miller's Profile

Socket23 is led by Joseph Miller, a GIAC-certified engineer focused on translating complex regulatory requirements into resilient security programs. Joseph blends deep technical experience with a practical mindset honed through real-world incident response, infrastructure design, and compliance engagements.

Our core team sets the strategy and delivery standards, while a trusted bench of contracted engineers allows us to surge capacity for specialized tasks on demand. Whether the work calls for DFIR analysts, cloud architects, or compliance writers, we deploy the right specialists without slowing projects down.

Socket23 operates out of Sandy, Oregon and supports clients throughout the United States with remote-first delivery and on-site options for critical milestones.
